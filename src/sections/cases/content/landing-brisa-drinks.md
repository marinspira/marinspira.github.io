Landing page project built for Brisa Drinks, focused on campaign performance, visual consistency, and lead capture.

[preview](#media-overview)

### Stack

- **UI:** Adobe XD and Next.js.
- **APIs:** REST and RD Station.

### Implementation

- Translated the Adobe XD layout into a responsive Next.js landing page.
- Built the interface with reusable sections to keep campaign content easier to maintain.
- Integrated REST services and RD Station for lead capture and marketing workflows.
- Kept the page lightweight and focused on conversion.

### Result

The landing page gave the campaign a dedicated, responsive experience connected to RD Station, making lead generation easier to track and operate.
